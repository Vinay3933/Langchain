# Using The Website

#### Get a quick introduction to the features available on midjourney.com by
watching the video below. Still have questions? Check out additional website

Your browser does not support the video element.

  

Many website features, including image creation and some organizational tools,
are only available to current Midjourney subscribers. New and returning users
can subscribe by visiting their Subscription Management page.

Some website features are not yet optimized for mobile browsers, and may not
be available on all devices.

  

Website image generation is currently only available as a limited test for
users with at least 100 Discord generations. To check your eligibility, use
the `/info` command wherever you generate images in Discord and look for your
"Lifetime Usage" count.Midjourney is an independent research lab exploring new mediums of thought and
